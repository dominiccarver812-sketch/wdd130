Idaho, USA

Idaho is a state in the western USA. It borders Utah to the southeast, Nevada to the southwest, Washington & Oregon to the west, Montana & Wyoming to the east, and Canada to the north. It is known for it's beautiful forests & landmarks, like the Craters of the Moon or Bear Lake. Idaho is also home to many wild forest animals like Bears, Wolves, Deer and Moose. The main capital, Boise, is home to over 200,000 people.
